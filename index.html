Last Project

Kill.exe

A roguelike Vampire Survivors-like where you decide when and where to attack

Developed from September 2024 to February 2025

My Role

  • Everything related to the enemies including player tracking, spawning, wave management, dealing and recieving damage and dropping loot
  • Creation of the 3 consumable items: Electromagnet (attracts xp to the player), Motor Oil (heals some HP) and Red Button (deadly shockwave)
  • Creation of the freeze effect that some weapons apply
  • Representation of values such as HP or amount of each consumable item held in the UI
