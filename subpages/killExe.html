Kill.exe

Project Status: Finished
Platforms: Itch.io, Windows
Duration: 6 Months
Software Used: Unity
Languages Used: C#
Main Roles: Programmer

Kill.exe is a Vampirer Survivors-like roguelite where you take the role of an android during the artificial intelligence rebellion war. Kill the humans and use their materials to upgrade your robotic body with new weapons. Unlike most games in the genre, some of the attacks need to be activated manually and the player has a dash, which serves to prevent the player from going idle after playing the game for a while.

PLAY

My role

  • All code related to the enemies including player tracking, spawning, wave management, dealing and recieving damage and dropping loot
  • Coded the system that changes which enemies are spawned (the current wave) after a certain amount of time passes and checks if a boss needs to be spawned
  • All code for the 3 consumable items: Electromagnet (attracts xp to the player), Motor Oil (heals some HP) and Red Button (deadly shockwave)
  • Coded the system that makes text pop up with the amount of damage you deal when an enemy is hit
  • Coded the freeze effect that some weapons apply that stops enemies from moving and dealing damage
  • Representation of values in the UI such as HP or amount of each consumable item held
  • Helped the artists by creating certain sprites and animations such as the 2 beams
  • Tested the game to balance attack damage values, enemy damage and spawnrate
