Break In

Project Status: Finished
Platforms: Itch.io
Duration: 2 Weeks
Software Used: Unity
Languages Used: C#
Main Roles: Programmer, Artist

Break In is a parody of the arcade game Break Out but you play as the ball instead of the paddle. The objective is to increase your score as high as you can by chaining block breaks and increasing the multiplier. The game was made for the 16th Pirate Software Game Jam with the theme "You are the weapon". According to the judges, the game was really fun to play but it did not follow the theme closely enough to reach the finals.

PLAY

My role

  • Coded all blocks and their special properties (Multiple hits to break, explode, give powerup, unbreakable, etc.)
  • Coded most of the ball movement and made it bounce
  • Coded the system that keeps track of your score, increases the multiplier and displays the increase as text on the screen
  • Coded all 3 powerups: Explosion (Destroys in 3x3 on next collision), Phantom (Passes through blocks until you touch air) and Duplicate (Creates a clone of the ball)
  • Coded the system that ends the level when all breakable blocks are broken
  • Made the art for all blocks and powerup icons
  • Designed the UI and game layout
